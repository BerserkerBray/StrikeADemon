How To Play

Objective: Survive

Controls: The player is moved with "A" (left) and "D" (right).
The player can also jump using "W",
this is necessary in order to dodge arrows.
The player can also attack using "Spacebar".

You have 10 hearts which will be lost each time you are hit by an attack,
if your health drops to 0, the game is over.

Fight well.
